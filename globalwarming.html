What is Global Wharming?

Global warming is the steady increase in Earth's average temperature, primarily caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation, which release greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.

What are the causes of Global Warming?

Global warming is caused by the increased concentration of greenhouse gases (like carbon dioxide and methane) in the atmosphere, primarily from burning fossil fuels for energy and transportation, deforestation, and certain agricultural practices.

What are the effects of global warming?

Hotter temperatures. As greenhouse gas concentrations rise, so does the global surface temperature.
